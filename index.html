Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship & Trust

Founded in 1910, Modern Furniture & Mattresses began as a small family workshop passionate about quality and design. Today, from our flagship showroom at 99 Jefferson St, we curate collections that blend timeless aesthetics with modern living.

Every piece we stock is tested for durability, comfort, and style. Our in-store design consultants help you create cohesive, beautiful spaces — no matter your budget or taste.
